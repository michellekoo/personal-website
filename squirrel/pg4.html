"CRASH, BURN, WOOSH!" The volcanoes exploded. Scurry yelped and screamed for his dear little life! Sally and Chester still managed to maintain their unwavering courage. Scurry was terrified that his small, little paws would burn in the lava. Tip-toeing, they all scurried past the lava as fast as they could to reach the next landmark of the race. Scurry's doubt really began to build up.
