Finally Sally, Chester, and Scurry reached the oasis of the National Squirrel Race! Washing, brushing, and cleaning their paws and fur, they all relaxed beneath the leafy tree and the cool, calm water. Scurry was really enjoying himself. He was so content with the oasis he never wanted to leave! Sally and Chester, however, would not allow Scurry to quit the race. "We started together, we end together!" they chimed.
